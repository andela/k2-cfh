ABOUT CARDS FOR HUMANITY

Cards for Humanity is a fast-paced online version of the popular card game, which is a platform where you have fun and have the opportunity to donate to children in need, while remaining as despicable and awkward as you naturally.

HOW TO PLAY THE GAME
A player is selected as the card "Czar" and is required to select a question card.
Cards for Humanity
Each player is presented with the question card selected by the card "Czar" and 10 answer cards.
Cards for Humanity
After answer submissions from players, the card "Czar" contemplate on the answer cards and select that which best answers the question.
Cards for Humanity
The player whose answer card has been selected by the "Czar" is announced the winners of that round, and the process is repeated.
